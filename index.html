About me

Hey there, I'm Curtis! I build things for the web because I like watching an idea turn into something people can actually click on. Somewhere between debugging a stubborn CSS bug and shipping a feature that just works, I found the part of coding that keeps me hooked.

Let's connect and create something amazing together!

  • Based in Leipzig, Germany — open to remote work, hybrid setups, or relocating for the right opportunity.

  • Always curious, always learning. New frameworks and tools don't scare me — they're just the next thing to figure out.

  • I like breaking problems down into small, solvable pieces — analytical thinking, a bit of creativity, and enough persistence to see it through.
